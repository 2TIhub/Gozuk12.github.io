About

ScorpionSquad is a Hypixel SkyBlock Guild related to mid/late game players. We offer Free Daily God Splashes with no fee or tax, to help the Guild Members progress faster, because players in mid game can't afford $100,000 God Splash.

There aren't many Guilds related for mid game players offering Free Daily God Splashes. Our Members don't have to spend single coin for any splash, which helps a lot in getting to late game.
